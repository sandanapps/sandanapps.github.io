Privacy Policy

How PogoDex handles your data

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: PogoDex does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from you to us. Your Pokémon collection data stays on your device. The app does display ads via Google AdMob, which may collect certain device identifiers as described below.

1. Who We Are

PogoDex is a free, independent Android application developed as a personal fan project. It is not affiliated with Nintendo, Game Freak, Creatures Inc., or The Pokémon Company International.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: sandan.apps@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

PogoDex itself does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not require you to create an account or log in.
  • We do not collect your name, email address, or any other personal details.
  • Your Pokémon collection progress (caught Pokémon, caught status) is stored locally on your device only using Android's Room database. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly use the Export feature to create a backup file.
  • We do not use crash reporting or analytics SDKs.

3. Advertising — Google AdMob

PogoDex displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. To serve and personalise ads, Google may collect and use:

  • Advertising ID (Google Advertising ID / GAID)
  • Device information (model, operating system version)
  • IP address (for approximate location / country)
  • App usage data and interaction with ads

This data is collected and processed by Google, not by us. It is used to display relevant advertisements and to prevent ad fraud.

Google's data practices are governed by Google's own Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google's advertising terms:
https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

Opting out of personalised ads: You can opt out of interest-based advertising on your Android device by going to Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID (Android 12+) or enabling Opt out of Ads Personalisation on older versions.

4. External Data Sources

The app fetches publicly available Pokémon data from the following third-party services. These requests are made over HTTPS and do not include any personal data:

  • PokéAPI (pokeapi.co) — Pokémon names, sprites, and metadata
  • GitHub (github.com) — app data files (JSON) hosted in a public repository
  • Pokémon GO Wiki / Fandom (pokemongo.fandom.com) — event and form Pokémon sprites

These services have their own privacy policies which govern how they handle network requests.

5. Permissions

The app requests the following Android permission:

  • INTERNET — required to fetch Pokémon data and sprites from external sources, and to load advertisements.

No other permissions (camera, microphone, contacts, location, storage) are requested.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All locally stored data (your Pokémon collection) can be deleted at any time by:

  • Going to Android Settings → Apps → PogoDex → Storage → Clear Data, or
  • Uninstalling the application.

We do not hold any of your data on our servers, so there is nothing for us to delete on our end.

7. Children's Privacy

PogoDex is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The advertising SDK (Google AdMob) is configured to serve non-personalised ads only when the app determines the user may be a child, in compliance with applicable laws (COPPA, GDPR).

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

9. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
sandan.apps@gmail.com
