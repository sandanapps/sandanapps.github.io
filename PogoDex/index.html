The dexes

A tracker for every part of your collection

Main Dex

Full Pokédex

Browse all Pokémon with real sprites from PokéAPI. Tap to cycle through Not caught → Regular → Shiny → Lucky. Filter by region (Kanto, Johto, …), search by name, and jump straight to uncaught.

Event Dex

Event & Costumed Pokémon

Never lose track of a costumed Pikachu or a Halloween Gengar. Sprites from the Pokémon GO Wiki. Two tabs: Regular and Shiny.

Form Dex

Regional & Alternate Forms

Alolan Vulpix, Galarian Slowpoke, Hisuian Arcanine — every alternate form has its own entry. Track regular and shiny variants independently.

Your data, your way

Export & Import your collection

All progress is stored locally on your device. Back it up anytime with a single tap.

📤 Export to JSON

Save your entire collection — all dexes, all states — to a .json file you can keep anywhere.

📥 Import & Restore

Moving to a new phone? Reinstalling? Import your backup and pick up exactly where you left off.

🔒 Privacy First

No account, no cloud, no tracking. Your data never leaves your device unless you share the file yourself.

⚡ Fresh Sprites

Pokémon data is fetched from PokéAPI on demand and cached locally for fast, offline browsing.
